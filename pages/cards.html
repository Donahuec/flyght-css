Overview

Cards are visual containers that can help build structured layouts and neatly contain information. They can be defined by applying the class fc-card to the desired element. Currently, cards are implemented to allow the following sub-sections:

  • Title: fc-card-title
  • Content: fc-card-content
  • Image: fc-card-image
  • Tabs: fc-card-tabs
  • Footer: fc-card-footer


This is a Generic Card

You can put whatever you want here.
<div class="fc-card">
  <div class="fc-card-title">
    <h4>
      This is a Generic Card
    </h4>
  </div>
  <div class="fc-card-content">
    You can put whatever you want here.
  </div>
</div>

Card Sections

Title

class="fc-card-title"

A section meant to hold a title for the card. Usually paired with a header tag. 

<div class="fc-card">
  <div class="fc-card-title">
    <h4>
      This is Title
    </h4>
  </div>
</div>

Content

class="fc-card-content"

A section for generic content. Generally the bulk of card content will be here. You can put whatever you want in content blocks. 

<div class="fc-card">
  <div class="fc-card-content">
    You can put whatever you want here.
  </div>
</div>

Image

class="fc-card-image"

A section for including images in a card. Used with img or figure elements. 

<div class="fc-card">
  <div class="fc-card-image">
    <img src="/assets/Moose.jpg" 
        alt="picture of Moose the cat" />
  </div>
</div>

Tabs

class="fc-card-tabs fc-tab-group"

A section for including tabs in your card. Pair the tab content with another card section. It is recommended to include all tab-content elements in one card section, to avoid any extra borders or artifacts. 

<div class="fc-card">
  <div class="fc-tab-group fc-primary fc-card-tabs">
    <button class="fc-tab-btn fc-tab-active">Tab One</button>
    <button class="fc-tab-btn">Tab Two</button>
  </div>
  <div class="fc-card-content">
    <div id="tab-one" class="fc-tab-content fc-tab-active">
      <p>
        Tab One Content
      </p>
    </div>
    <div id="tab-two" class="fc-tab-content">
      <p>
        Tab Two Content
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Footer

class="fc-card-footer"

A section for including a footer. Good for additional notes or action buttons. Will automatically pull towards the bottom of it's available space. Designed to be used as the last element in the card. 

<div class="fc-card">
<div class="fc-card-content">
  <p>
    Some interesting content here
  </p>
</div>
<div class="fc-card-footer">
  This is a footer.
</div>
</div>

Customizing Cards

class="fc-card fc-flat"

You can remove the card shadow by adding the fc-flat class.

class="fc-card fc-border-{color}"

You can change the border colors with the fc-border-{color} classes, described in colors.

class="fc-card fc-bg-{color}"

You can Change the background color of the card with the fc-bg-{color} classes, described in colors.

class="fc-card-content fc-bg-{color}"

Background colors can also be applied to individual sections. Mix and match!
